How it all started!

Lava Ghimire was originally from Kathmandu, Nepal. She was "hired" as an assiatant cook for her dad during sundays at home. This is where her passion grew. In the kitcehn of her home, it only took a few years for her to advance to making food on her own and then, in 2007, was promoted to become Chef of Sunday Breakfast in the Ghimire Estate. In 2015, Ghimire's sister Bala Ghimire became Vegan. There was a problem. Ghimire didnt know how to make anything vegan.

Therefore, Ghimire started experimenting with diffrent ingredients. The result was sug·ar is a bakery that focuses on people with gluten, dairy, egg, and soy sensitivities.For those who are the health-minded, but most importantly, those with allergies, who are often unable to indulge.

Lava Ghimire, Founder & CEO of sug·ar
